Play the Wavelength game!

How to play:

  1. At least 2 players are necessary
  2. Click the statement button first to get your prompt
  3. Close the fan and then press the click to spin button
  4. Player 1 will then open the fan to see the guessing value, while Player 2 looks away
  5. Player 1 can then close the fan and then start giving the guess which they believe matches the number based on the category given
  6. Player 2 can type in what they believe the actual number spun is
  7. Player 2 can then record how many points they got from the round and then switch turns with Player 1
  8. The player with the most points wins after 5 rounds
